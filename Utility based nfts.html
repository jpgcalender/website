Utility NFTs - The Power of Unique Tokens

What are Utility NFTs?

Utility NFTs are distinctive digital tokens that stand for a particular purpose or utility, such as gaining access to a good, service, or group of people. These tokens can be used to establish ownership, track advantages, and grant entry to special events and experiences.

Examples of Utility NFTs

Here are a few examples of utility-based NFTs:

Rari Capital

Rari Capital is a DeFi platform that uses NFTs to represent membership in its liquidity pools. These NFTs grant users access to the platform's products and services, as well as a share of the platform's profits.

Bored A.I.

Bored A.I. is an AI-powered art project that creates unique pieces of art for its users. It uses NFTs to represent ownership of these unique works of art. The NFTs not only prove ownership, but they also allow users to access exclusive features such as the ability to vote on new art themes and styles.

The Sandbox

The Sandbox is a virtual gaming platform that uses NFTs to represent ownership of virtual real estate within the game. These NFTs grant players access to their own virtual space, as well as the ability to monetize their creations and experiences within the game.
